March 2016

Sense Memories at Pro Arts Gallery // Electric Objects Interview

Sense Memories are on view in the Project Space at Pro Arts Gallery in Oakland through April 9 as part of Hybrid Series.

Electric Objects interviewed me in conjunction with the release of my Art Club collection, Predictions.

February 2016

Electric Objects Art Club

Electric Objects commissioned this Art Club collection for the EO1, their internet-connected display for digital art. Predictions are still and video works that confront the future of the landscape. I thought of bunker life and the view through a clouded window; appreciating natural resources in memoriam; and how we might adorn our homes amidst altered and damaged wild places.

November 2015

#futureart

My sense memory A Tipping Point won Newhive and Fusion's #futureart open call, and was exhibited here in SF at the Real Future Fair.

November 2015

Winterfest Art Show

I donated a vaguely street-themed woodcut to the San Francisco Bicycle Coalition for their Winterfest Art Show and Auction, which raised nearly $10,000 for better biking in San Francisco.
