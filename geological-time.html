Dioramas installation at Black & White Projects, 2017 // Exhibited at Print Screen Festival, 2016 // Artist diary in PAPER Magazine // Cut- and fold-your-own GIFs in Living Room Light Exchange 01 // Commission for Electric Objects, 2016 // geologicaltime.net for Internet! A Retrospective, 2016

Aspects of Geological Time is an internet-based project about the long scale of the universe. The title comes from my fascination with California's visually evident seismic qualities. It's comforting to be a small part of a large system, and galvanizing as we face the realities of climate change. Through drawn elements and the inherent loop of an animated GIF, I suggest the cyclical, therefore endless, nature of the landscape and our infinitely divisible attention to it.
