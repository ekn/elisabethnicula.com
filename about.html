Bio

Elisabeth Nicula is an artist and writer in San Francisco. She has two publishing projects: San Francisco Review of Whatever, a print magazine for critical writing on art and other subjects; and Smooth Friend, a small press for new short fiction and poetry. She amassed over 100,000 photographs of two California scrub jays during a five-year period of deep observation and friendship.
