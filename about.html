About

Elisabeth Nicula is an artist and writer from Norfolk, Virginia. She is interested in seeing and being seen by the non-human world, abstracted scales of space and time, poetics, and memory. Smooth Friend is her publishing project.

Contact

Send inquiries to hi@elisabethnicula.com.
