Electric Objects commissioned this Art Club collection for the EO1, their internet-connected display for digital art. Predictions are still and video works that confront the future of the landscape. I thought of bunker life and the view through a clouded window; appreciating natural resources in memoriam; and how we might adorn our homes amidst altered and damaged wild places. Read more, and look at some GIFs and influences, in this interview.
